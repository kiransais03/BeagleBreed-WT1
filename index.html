The Beagle Blog

The Beagle is a breed of small scent hound, similar in appearance to the much larger foxhound.The beagle was developed primarily for hunting hare, known as beagling. Posssessing a great sense of smell and superior tracking instincts,the beagle is the primary breed used as a detection dog for prohibited agricultural imports and foodstuffs in quarantine aroung the world. The beagle is intelligent and is a popular pet due to its size, good temper, and a lack of inherited health problems.

Trivia

  4. Colour -Tricolour or white in combination with black & tan/brown or brown/tan
